PROJECTS

PROJECTS

  • Blueshift

    A cooperative top-down shooter with survival elements about a space pilot after crashing on an unknown planet. Uses Unity's UNET library for networked multiplayer. Made in Unity 5 and Autodesk Maya.

  • Near Field VR

    1st Place Winner, SIGGRAPH 2015 Immersive Realities Contest. Uses a series of lightfield images to render realistic objects in Virtual Reality with minimal geometry. Made with Unity 4, Oculus DK2, and Phasespace Motion Tracking System.

  • Elementerra

    A Virtual Reality sandbox game where players sculpt the world as the nature spirit using a palette and brush to manipulate, destroy, and grow plants and terrain. Made using Untiy 4, Oculus DK2, and Razer Hydras.

  • 2D Line of Sight

    A script that simulates line of sight from a top-down 2D perspective by generating a mesh to block unseen areas. Works with Unity's built in box and polygon colliders. Made in Unity 5.

  • You Were Born To Do This

    48-Hour 2015 Global Game Jam entry. A game where two players try to figure out what to do next. Made in Unity 4.

  • Toppletarian

    24-Hour "Fall" Game Jam entry. A game about swinging from a statue of a dictator, attacking and destroying it to score points. Made in Unity 4.
